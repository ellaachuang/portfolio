PADDLE ANALYSIS APP INTERFACE

figma wireframes




For the NYU Tandon Concrete Canoe Vertically Integrated Project Team, I designed the wireframes for a mobile app that allows users to track their paddling performance. It inputs information gained from an Apple Watch and an iPhone strapped to the athlete's arm to analyze the paddler's stroke rate, stroke length, and other metrics. I designed both iPhone and Apple Watch interfaces so the app works seamlessly between the two devices.
