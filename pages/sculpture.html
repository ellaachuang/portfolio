HEART LOCK PROJECT

six-foot tall, stainless steel sculpture to place locks on




I created a 6-foot tall metal heart sculpture that people can put locks on to symbolize togetherness. Donors will receive engraved plates to lock in place on the grid of the sculpture, creating a mosaic of my community. It stands in the courtyard of a local theater and will help support its performances. I learned about welding, logical thinking, and problem-solving. To me, this sculpture represents unity through creativity and art. I found that inspiration is not found by searching for it, but by living an exciting life that will lead you to it and being in a mindset where creativity is inevitable.
